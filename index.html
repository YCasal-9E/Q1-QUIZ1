Famous Philippine Historical Figures

Jose Rizal Jose Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda 1861-1896
  • June 19, 1861- He was born in Calamba, Laguna as the seventh child of Francisco Mercado Rizal and Teodora Alonso Realonda
  • March 21, 1887- Copies of Noli Me Tangere made available
  • September, 1891, 1891- El Filibusterismo was published
  • July 3, 1892- He founded La Liga Filipina
  • August 28, 1893- Exiled in Dapitan
  • December 30, 1896- Execution
  • Doctor
  • Writer
    • Notable works: Noli Me Tangere, El Filibusterismo, To the Filipino Youth, Mi Ultimo Adios
  • Founder of the nationalist secret society, La Liga Filipina
Andres Bonifacio Andres Bonifacio y de Castro 1863-1897
  • November 30, 1863- He was born in Manila as the first of six children of Catalina de Castro and Santiago Bonifacio
  • July 7, 1892- He founded the Katipunan
  • September 28, 1892- He gets married to Gregoria De Jesus
  • May 10, 1897- He was sentenced to death
  • "Father of the Philippine Revolution"
  • founded the secret revolutionary movement "Katipunan"
Emilio Aguinaldo Emilio Aguinaldo y Famy 1869-1964
  • March 22, 1869- He was born in Cavite
  • January 3, 1899- term as a president
  • February 6, 1964- death at the age of 94
  • declared Philippine independence
  • First president of the Philippines
Apolinario Mabini Apolinario Mabini y Maranan 1864-1903
  • July 23, 1864- He was born in Talaga, Tanauan, Batangas
  • May 13, 1903- death due to cholera at the age of 38
  • Prime Minister
  • "Brains of the Revolution"
Marcelo H. del Pilar Marcelo Hilario del Pilar Gatmaitan 1850-1896
  • August 30, 1850- He was born in Bulacan
  • July 4, 1896- He died at the age of 45
  • Writer
  • Lawyer
  • Founder of Diariong Tagalog
